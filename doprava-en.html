Transport and Parking


Since Medvědí bouda is located in the Krkonoše National Park, parking spaces are very limited. Parking is therefore mainly possible in the designated parking lot at Medvědí koleno (see map below). From there, it's about a 2-kilometer walk to Medvědí bouda. Transportation of guests and luggage (or just luggage) from Medvědí koleno to Medvědí bouda will be arranged.

If for any reason you still need to be granted entry permission, please don't forget to mention it in the questionnaire.



Accommodation


You will be able to rest your head directly at Medvědí bouda, which provides shelter for up to 145 guests. The lodge offers accommodation for less demanding guests in cozy, but not luxurious rooms. Nevertheless, we believe that after a day full of celebrations, dancing, and mountain air, you will sleep there just as well as in a ten-star hotel.

If you're interested in learning more about Medvědí bouda, visit their website.

If you are interested in accommodation, please also let us know in our questionnaire.
